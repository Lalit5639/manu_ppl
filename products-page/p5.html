MONO - POTASH (Potash In Form Of KMB) Crystal

Information

Technical: Potash inform of KMB with base of Silicone, Crystalized Ammonium sulphate, Crystalized Manganese sulphate and Potassium Chloride.

Packing: 50 Kg Bag

Source:Developed By PPL. Made in India

USPs

  • Mono Potash helps to mobilize the insoluble form of Potassium for Crops to Growth at a faster Rate, provides resistance to biotic and a biotic stress ,increase nitrogen uptake to plants and prevent the leaching of nutrients.
  • Mono Potash has a structural base of silicone which helps to stuck the moisture in upper layer of soil to prevent the leaching of nutrients with water under the soil and also reduce irrigation water consumption.

Dosage

50kg/Acre (Soil)
