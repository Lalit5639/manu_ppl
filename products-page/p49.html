TRIKON (Tricontanol - 0.1% EW)

Information

Technical: Tricontanol - 0.1% EW

Packing: 250 ml, 500 ml & 1 litre

Source: Developed by PPL. Made in India

USPs

  • Auxins are plant hormones that control the growth and development of plants.
  • Tricontanol helps to regulate the levels of auxins in plants, which can lead to increases cell elongation and division, resulting in larger and stronger plants.

Dosage

1-1.5 ml / Litre (Foliar)
