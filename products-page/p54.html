RAISER-90 (Crop Yield Promoter, Spreader, Activator, Wetter)

Information

Technical: Highly concentrated non-ionic adjuvant with 90% active ingredients

Packing: 100 ml, 250 ml, 500 ml, 1 L & 5 L

Source: Developed by PPL. Made in India

USPs

  • RAISER-90 is an excellent Spreader, Activator, and Wetter.
  • RAISER-90 increases crop yield by improving pesticide coverage.
  • It activates the spray fluid to moisten the plant surface and allows uniform spreading of spray deposits.
  • a helpful tool in irrigation, it improves the water absorption in soil it helps and facilitates uniform mixing of powders, pesticides and most liquid fertilizers
  • It is compeletely non-Phytotoxic, safe and eco-friendly. use in fungicides herbicides, insecticides, foliar fertilizers plant nutrients and defoliators

Dosage

  • 250 ml / Acre (Soil / Drip)
  • 0.5-1 ml / litre (Foliar)
