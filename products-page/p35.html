CNB+

Information

Technical: Calcium - 15% / Nitrogen — 10% / Boron — 3% (SC)

Packing: 500 ml & 1 litre

Source: Developed by PPL. Made in India

USPs

  • CNB+ reduce early (Premature) fruit drop, whilst giving healthy fruit development, Improving fruit quality and its shelf life.
  • Fully compatible with other pesticides, fungicides and other fertilizers.
  • It improve the size and control the shape and stop the blast stage during mature stage of crop.

Dosage

2 ml / Litre( Foliar)
