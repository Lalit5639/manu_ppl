PHINO - COP (Copper Sulphate)

Information

Technical: Copper - (24%) and Sulphur - (12%) (Crystal/ Granular)

Packing:500 gm(Crystal) & 3 kg(Granular)

Source:Developed by PPL. Made in India

USPs

  • Copper Sulphate is used both as a fertilizer and a fungicide material.
  • It promotes seed production and formation.
  • It plays an essential role in chlorophyll formation.

Dosage

  • 3 Kg/ Acre(Soil)
  • 2 - 2.5 Gm / Litre(Foliar)
