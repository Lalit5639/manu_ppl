About us

We started our journey in 2019 with a vision to make a transforming impact in agriculture society. We observed the problems and issues of the basic Agriculture System.

We address the challenge of farming in typical rural areas and started working to find the solutions from the grass-roots level. At first, we marked the challenges of farmers that were stopping them from uplifting their farming culture.

After this, we addressed the problems of the whole retail channel, which is forcing them to stick to traditional business practices. These practices are outdated and have lower financial returns. We acknowledged the network across the country and found significant trouble in terms of core fertilizer production and distribution stations.

We worked with farmers, retailers, and traders in multiple geographical areas. After gathering all this data, we stabilized Phoenix Plants Lifesaver Pvt Ltd with a vision to make a transforming impact in Agriculture Society.

We developed a unique range of products and services in core fertilizers segment under the FCO guidelines of the Indian Government. We spread our services across various geographical areas, and found our concept and services can make a huge impact.

Currently, we fulfill a major part of core fertilizers needed in Northern India, and are committed to continuous improvement through our R&D division, equipped with modern experimental facilities.

We look forward to developing more qualified quality control management and establishing strict adherence to all quality norms, ensuring delivery of top-quality products and services.

We promise to give a faithful, uplifting, and sustainable service to the National Agriculture Society.

Mission and Vision

We are going to transform the whole service channel of chemical fertilizers in Indian Agriculture system.

Which is running from decades on the same patterns and terminology. Our Mission is to provide the quality and productivity to the farmers with our products and services without challenging their beliefs, delivering faith and internal business opportunities to the whole established service channel, which they deserve and never got before due to the current service system.

We are committed to change the stacies and reliability on imported chemical fertilizers and stabilize an "Made in India" service systems under the norms and approach of FCO and Indian Government. This will express our passion towards transforming this Agriculture system in INDIA.
